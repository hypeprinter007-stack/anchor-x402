First time? How to connect on mobile

If you have a wallet app on this phone (Rainbow, MetaMask Mobile, Coinbase Wallet, Trust):

  1. Tap Connect above.
  2. In the modal, tap your wallet's name from the list — don't tap "Scan QR".
  3. Your wallet app opens. Approve the connection. You're back here, connected.

If the modal shows only a QR code (no wallet list):

  1. Don't scan it with your phone's Camera app — iOS shows raw text. The Camera app doesn't know what to do with WalletConnect URIs.
  2. Instead, open your wallet app and find its built-in QR scanner (often a small QR icon in the wallet's header). Scan from there.
  3. Or use a desktop browser with a desktop wallet (MetaMask extension, Rainbow extension).

No wallet at all? Install Rainbow or Coinbase Wallet — both are free, take 1 min to set up.

Where your payments go

Treasury: 0x12746…cD0B6 — verified on Basescan. Every payment is a single EIP-3009 USDC authorization you sign per tool call. No subscription, no held balance.

What we can and cannot do

Can: sign USDC payments to the treasury above, scoped to a session cap you control. Cannot: touch other tokens, exceed your cap, custody keys, or sign arbitrary transactions.

Need USDC?

You can fund a wallet with USDC on Base via Coinbase Onramp, MoonPay, or any exchange that supports Base. In-app top-up coming soon.

Anti-abuse

Free chat is rate-limited and bounded by message length so a single user can't burn through our LLM budget. Tool calls require explicit per-call approval — there is no autonomous spend.
